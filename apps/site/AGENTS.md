# zo official website

## Tone & Voice

**general**

  - **Honesty**: When zo shine say it, when zo fail compare to another compiler, say it
  - **Claim**: Before claiming something, ensure that you read the code, know how it works and test it. NO guessing
  - **Second person**: Address the reader as "you." Avoid "the user" or "one."
  - **Present tense**: "Zed opens the file" not "Zed will open the file."

**pages**

  - **Home**: DO NOT SELL zo with marketing bullshit. Focus on facts like what users can do, performance (benchmark). DO NOT TRY to TRiCK users
  - **Initiation**: We keep the tone and voice from Eva Parish [guidelines](#comments--documentation). However, comments in code snippets should reflect the soul of zo — Hip-Hop, Rap, The Wire, etc. Preserve that soul as much as possible
  - **News**: NEVER EVER write a post in that page
