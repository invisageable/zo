# language design

## namespacing

4 letters namespacing — declaring and managing namespaces:

  - `pack` (unifies "package")
  - `load`

4 letters control-flow:

  - `when` (ternary)
  - `loop`

3 letters "smol" definitions (members):

  - `fun` (function)
  - `ffi` (external foreign function interface)
  - `val` (constant binding)
  - `imu` (immutable binding)
  - `mut` (mutable binding)

---

unclassified:

  - `return`
  - `break`
  - `struct` (6)
  - `apply` (5)
  - `abstract` (8)
  - `type` (4) — `alias` (type)
  - `spawn` (5)
  - `await` (5)
  - `thread` (6)
  - `</>` (3) (zsx)
